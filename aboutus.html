Our Story

Discover the journey that brought us to where we are today

Designing Spaces, Creating Experiences

Founded in 2010, Design Space Interiors has grown from a small design studio to one of Kenya's leading interior design firms. Our passion for creating beautiful, functional spaces has driven us to deliver exceptional designs for both residential and commercial clients.

We believe that great design should not only look beautiful but also enhance the way people live and work. Our team of talented designers and craftsmen work closely with clients to understand their needs and bring their vision to life.

With offices in Nairobi and Mombasa, we've completed over 200 projects across East Africa, each one reflecting our commitment to quality, innovation, and client satisfaction.
