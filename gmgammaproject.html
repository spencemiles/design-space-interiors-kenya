About the GM Gamma Project

The GM Gamma Project represents a new standard in industrial facility design in Nairobi. Our challenge was to create a space that would maximize production efficiency while prioritizing worker safety and comfort in a challenging industrial environment.

"This facility has exceeded all our operational expectations. The thoughtful design has improved workflow efficiency by 35% while significantly enhancing our team's working conditions."

- Manufacturing Director

We focused on creating zones that support different manufacturing processes while maintaining flexibility for future adaptations. The integration of advanced ventilation and lighting systems creates an environment that supports both precision work and employee well-being.

The material palette combines industrial-grade durability with thoughtful ergonomic design. Smart lighting adjusts to different work areas and tasks, while modular workstations allow for easy reconfiguration as production needs evolve. The result is a facility that meets current needs while being prepared for future growth.
