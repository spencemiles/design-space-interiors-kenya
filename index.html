About Us

With nearly two decades of experience, we've perfected the art of creating spaces that inspire and delight.

Kenya's Premier Interior Design Firm

DesignSpace Interiors Ltd was founded in 2015 and is a leading interior design firm in Kenya, renowned for delivering exceptional commercial interior architecture and finish solutions. We have partnered with corporates, SMEs, government agencies, embassies, and startups across the country to create inspiring spaces that meet their unique needs.

At DesignSpace Interiors, we bring your vision to life with innovative and customized interior design solutions. Based in Nairobi, Kenya, we specialize in transforming both workspaces and living spaces to inspire creativity, productivity, and comfort. Our approach integrates modern design principles with the unique requirements of each client, ensuring that every project is both functional and aesthetically pleasing.
